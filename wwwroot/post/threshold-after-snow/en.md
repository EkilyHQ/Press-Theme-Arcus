# Threshold After Snow

![Threshold After Snow](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Special:FilePath/Snow_on_steps.jpg)

Snow is an honest reviewer. It shows where people actually step, where meltwater sits, and which beautiful edge becomes a maintenance complaint.

The entry performed well except at the inside corner of the planter, where the snow line proved the broom could not reach. We will soften that corner before the final masonry package.

A winter note makes the demo feel lived-in because architecture is never finished by photography. Weather keeps editing it.
