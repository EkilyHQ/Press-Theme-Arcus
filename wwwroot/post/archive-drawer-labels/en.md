# Archive Drawer Labels

![Archive Drawer Labels](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Special:FilePath/Architectural_drawings_archive.jpg)

We renamed the archive drawers by question instead of project: stairs, thresholds, borrowed light, outdoor rooms, unresolved corners.

The change is small, but it altered how the team searches. Someone designing a bench no longer needs to remember which house had the good bench.

A studio archive should be usable before it becomes historical. Labels are infrastructure for memory.
