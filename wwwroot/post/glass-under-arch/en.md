# The Specimen Case Under the Arch

![The Specimen Case Under the Arch](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Special:FilePath/Butterfly_Under_Glass_Drawing.jpg)

A specimen case is a tiny building. It has a base, a cover, a viewing rule, and a strong opinion about distance.

I placed this note in the demo because Arcus should make quiet archival material feel intentional. The rounded containers and low-contrast outlines help old line art sit naturally beside contemporary prose.

The object is fragile, but the page should not feel precious. It should simply say: look here, then look again.
