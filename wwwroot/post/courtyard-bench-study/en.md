# Courtyard Bench Study No. 3

![Courtyard Bench Study No. 3](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Special:FilePath/Garden_bench_in_courtyard.jpg)

Bench three finally touched the wall without becoming part of it. That was the missing proportion. The seat now feels permanent, but the cushion can still disappear in winter.

We kept the back low because the courtyard already has enough edges: brick, glass, planter, drain, threshold. The bench should gather people without drawing another boundary.

For the site, this post gives Arcus a quiet object study that sits between project essay and material note.
