# Tile Sample Meeting, Five Minutes Late

![Tile Sample Meeting, Five Minutes Late](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Special:FilePath/Portuguese_tiles.jpg)

The supplier sent the wrong blue. It was too green, too glossy, and completely useful. Everyone immediately understood what the kitchen should avoid.

A bad sample can shorten a meeting when it makes the boundary visible. We placed it beside the white oak, then beside the soapstone, then removed it. The room improved every time.

Decision: use a quieter handmade white tile, keep the grout warm, and let the range hood carry the only blue-grey note.
