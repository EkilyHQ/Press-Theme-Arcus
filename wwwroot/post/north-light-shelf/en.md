# North Light Shelf Prototype

![North Light Shelf Prototype](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Special:FilePath/Interior_with_window_and_light.jpg)

The first shelf mockup looked too architectural in the bad sense: a detail asking to be noticed. The second mockup did its job and disappeared.

We measured the winter line at three times of day and found that the shelf only needed to carry light eight feet, not the full room. That made the section thinner and the cost easier to defend.

The note belongs in the demo because real studio sites are full of these middle decisions. A detail is rarely heroic, but it can make the next twenty mornings better.
