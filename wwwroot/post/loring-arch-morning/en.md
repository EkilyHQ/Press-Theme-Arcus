# Morning Inside the Loring Arch

![Morning Inside the Loring Arch](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Special:FilePath/Loring_Arch_Interior.jpg)

The first hour inside the arch is never about scale. It is about repetition: rib, shadow, rib, shadow, a cadence that lets the eye walk ahead before the feet do.

Arcus renders this kind of note well because its rail and content surfaces stay calm. The theme leaves enough air around captions, lists, and small metadata for the architecture to feel measured rather than catalogued.

I keep returning to the end of the span, where the curve compresses into a rectangle of light. A threshold can be generous and exact at the same time.
